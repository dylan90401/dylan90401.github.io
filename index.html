About Me

Hello I'm Dylan Hollis Frontend Developer Cv Contact Me

I grew up in southern California where I ate drank and slept computers. I made my first computer program when I was 13 and my first BBS website when I was 16.

I attended and graduated the University California San Diego a Bachelors of Fine Arts with High Honors in 1999. Shortly afterward I attended Art Institute of California Los Angeles where I studied Interactive Engineering.

I also have an MCSA with nine MCP certifications. With plenty of acedemics and certifications I started working on websites during the dial up era and have never looked back. I have had stints as a HD-DVD developer, Blu Ray developer and Sr. Applications Developer for the National Football League. My favorite medium is working on enterprise level websites and applications.

I am currently recovering from a severe illness that has disabled my ability to work for the last few years. I am retraining myself to be even better at web application development than I ever was before. I look forward to putting my web developer skills to work in the near future and speaking about future opportunities.
